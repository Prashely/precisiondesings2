Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How much does a website cost?

Our custom website projects start from only R1300 for a basic, professional one-page site. Pricing scales depending on complexity, features (e.g., e-commerce, custom integrations), and the package you choose. We offer clear, fixed quotes upfront.

2. Do I need to pay monthly for the website?

It depends on the package you select. For basic, simple sites, you only pay the one-time build fee. However, most clients opt for a small monthly fee that covers essential services like hosting, security updates, and routine maintenance to ensure your site stays fast and secure.

3. How long does it take for my website to be done?

Standard website delivery typically takes between 5 to 14 business days from the kick-off meeting, assuming all necessary content (text, image, branding) is provided promptly. Larger or more complex projects may take longer, but we always prioritize speed.

4. Can my website be updated after it's done?

Yes, absolutely! We build all websites on easy-to-use platforms, allowing you to manage simple content updates yourself. For larger changes, technical updates, or ongoing strategy support, we offer flexible maintenance and support packages.
